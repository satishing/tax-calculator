A workbench for the tax practitioner — to project the exact tax an employer should deduct at source on a bonus, before and after the payout, with the surcharge cliff and 87A marginal relief modelled per the Income-Tax Act 2025.

What this tool does

  1. Computes annual TDS without the bonus and with the bonus across full salary, deductions, surcharge & cess.
  2. Isolates the incremental TDS — what your employer withholds in the month the bonus lands.
  3. Shows the effective tax rate on the bonus — exposing surcharge cliffs where the marginal rate can spike past 60%.
  4. Models the full deduction tree per IT Act 2025 — Section 11 (formerly 10), Chapter VIII (formerly VI-A) and the new section numbering for 80C → 123, 80D → 126, etc.
  5. Compares New vs Old regime side-by-side; either is selectable in the calculator.
  6. Provides 12 pre-computed bracket × bonus scenarios (₹20 L / ₹60 L / ₹1 Cr × varying bonuses).

Tier
Read this — Scenario 8 (CTC ₹1 Cr + Bonus ₹5 L) shows an effective bonus rate of about 62%. This is the surcharge cliff — adding ₹5 L to a ₹99.25 L taxable income crosses ₹1 Cr, which lifts the entire surcharge tier from 10% to 15%, applied to the full tax bill, not just the marginal income. The fix is either (a) accept it, (b) defer the bonus to keep income under ₹1 Cr, or (c) split the bonus across two FYs.
